MOTION TO DISMISS — EXCERPT (illustrative sample for the wa-cite-check demo)

The defendant was charged under RCW 9A.52.070. As the Washington Supreme Court
held in State v. Smith, 199 Wn.2d 1 (2022), unlawful entry requires proof of
intent to commit a crime against a person or property therein.

See also State v. Jones, 150 Wn.2d 50 (2003). The State further relies on
State v. Other, 999 P.3d 9 (2015), for the proposition that consent is an
affirmative defense.

Defendant cites State v. Fabricated, 404 Wn.2d 1 (2099), for the contrary view.
